About

This website is about everything afrobeat related. The songs, artists, history and all the original kings and queens who started afrobeats. Many people seem to forget that burnaboy, wizkid and tems arent the only afropop artists around. The reason they are most well known is because the western populaton has adapted their songs and therefore broadcasted it. There are many other afrobeat artists that have been ruling the music industry in africa for a long time, the purpose of this website is to recognize those artists and give them the recognition they deserve. My website is strictly for afro beat and nothing more,afrobeats are full of vibes and different beats. They arent all the same and they do come in different forms and can most definetly fit into other genres.

Goal

The goal of my webste is for more people to recognize afrobeats and branch out of the current afrobeat artists they listen too. Explore more artists and experience new music because i guarantee you there are many other underrated afrobeat artists out there better than the ones that we have today.
